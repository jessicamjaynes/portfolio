About Pennington Painting & More LLC

Pennington Painting & More LLC is a family-owned and operated construction business dedicated to providing high-quality work with a personal touch. We believe that every project, from a small painting job to a full home remodel, deserves our full attention and commitment to excellence.

Our team is passionate about transforming spaces and bringing our clients' visions to life. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, reliable service, and a strong work ethic. We are fully licensed and insured, ensuring peace of mind for every job we undertake.
